Good Governance is related to the public institutions that handles the public events, affairs, and resources. They are expected to be aware of the public situations and will be able to prevent any of the damages that could happen. They should be capable of being open minded to have thoughts and give advice that will make the people happy and organized. The public institutions should be smart for them to know what is wrong or if ever what is happening in public is not right anymore. In this case, the public institutions will be confident enough with their plans to implement it and will make sure that it will do good for the people. The people will notice immediately or eventually the changes of the plans implemented and it will show if it has a good effect with the public affairs.

How accountable is a Good Governance? Being accountable is the root requirement to the way of good governance. They are expected to have the right decisions despite all the negative things people will say against them. People will expect in them based on their judgments. Their decisions should consider the concerns of the people and will be able to do something about it. They should make sure that what they are planning about is for the betterment of the community. The local government is obliged to report about what is the outcome of their decisions and will be responsible for the consequences of what their decisions has made on behalf of their community.

How effective is good governance? The local government should be able to decide whether people and their resources are included in the decision-making process. They should include everything that is considered for the requirement. They should follow the process that will make their time worth it and will have possible good results for the community.

How inclusive is good governance? The local government should consider all interests of their community. They should consider all concerns of the people in the decision-making process. All types of groups should be included to have opportunities to state their concerns and considerations regarding their interest for the decision-making.

How does good governance participate?
The people who are interested in the decision-making should be given the chance to participate in the process. They will be provided with the information needed in order for them to give opinions of how to improve. They will be able to contribute for them to understand each other and consider its recommendation. The local government will be able to participate with the concerns of the people.

How responsive is good governance? The local government should be able to meet the needs of the entire community to be able to be of service to them. They should offer their help in order for the community to think positively against them and their environment. The community will also be able to help each other if the local government will be an example to them. People will follow their decisions if they will see it clearly that they are right and following them is better than opinion of others. The local government should know or foresee what the community deserves.

How transparent is good governance? The public institution will be able to make their people follow and understand the decision-making process by the way how they explain what has happened for the previous years to the present. They know that they are responsible to show how they will think of a way to make situations better. They will not make things complicated and they will just explain the way how people will see it clearly why they have made the decision instead. The people should be able to understand it the way how the public institution would want them to understand.
